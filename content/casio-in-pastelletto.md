_Casio in pastelletto_ means roughly "cheese treat" in vernacular Italian.

It is a medieval recipe and the taste changes a lot depending on the cheese you choose, so there are no exact quantities for the ingredients, and the dish may be considered a main one or a sweet (I think it was probably a dessert, though).
You will probably have to do some trial and error before you get a good result.

## Ingredients

- 2 slice cheese. Use a cheese that is mid-aged, not too savoury and not too delicate.
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Place the cheese in a pan with frying butter or lard.
1. Immediately sprinkle sugar and cinnamon all over it.
1. As soon as it softens, remove it from the pan.
1. Serve warm.
